Yayasan Daya Diri Scholarship

We provide scholarships to develop well-rounded students that will enhance the pool of post-SPM students to qualify for various esteemed undergraduate degree programmes in Malaysia or overseas.

Yayasan Daya Diri in a Nutshell

YDD aims to create opportunities for individuals and communities to excel by providing a range of scholarships that covers secondary education up to executive education, and support for community development, technical and vocational training, and general welfare programmes in Malaysia.

All You Need to Know: Yayasan Daya Diri

  • Art and Design
  • Business and Finance
  • Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Science and Engineering (not including Medicine)
  • Malaysian citizen
  • Must possess strong academic, extracurricular, and personal achievements
  • Have gained at least conditional admission to relevant institution
  • Fulfil other required personal traits and selection criteria.
  • Scanned copy of Passport Photo, NRIC, Birth Certificate
  • Scanned verified copy of academic transcript (Pre-University, or Diploma programme)*
  • Scanned verified copy of examination results slips (SPM/ other related examinations)*
  • Scanned copy of certificates/ testimonials of extra co-curricular activites
  • Scanned verified copy of Income Declaration documents
*All must be Certified True Copy
* All should be in PDF form

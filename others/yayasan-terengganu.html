Yayasan Terengganu Scholarship

Yayasan Terengganu Scholarship aims to attract top Terengganu talents in realising their vision to achieve sustainable growth and prosperity for the state of Terengganu .

Yayasan Terengganu Scholarship in a Nutshell

Yayasan Terengganu Scholarship is a study loan provided by the state to assist students in their third-tier education.

All You Need to Know: Yayasan Terengganu Scholarship

  • Foundation
  • A-Level
  • Matriculation
  • Asasi
    Foundation / A-Level :
  • Foundation / A Level applicant must pass SPM 2019 with minimum Grade A (A/A+) in at least 8 subjects including 3 Pure Science subjects (Biology, Chemistry and Physics), Mathematics, Additional Mathematics, Bahasa Melayu and English
  • Offer for overseas degree if applicant obtained excellent results in Foundation/A-Level, minimum requirement of at least 2A1B / CGPA 3.75 or more
  • Received an offer from recognised university by the government ESPECIALLY IN Science / Engineering / Accounting which is also approved by Yayasan Terengganu
    • Matriculation / Asasi :
  • Passed SPM 2019 with minimum Grade A (A-,A, or A+) in at least 8 subjects including 3 Pure Science subjects (Biology, Chemistry and Physics), Mathematics, Additional Mathematics, Bahasa Melayu and English
  • Received an offer letter from any programme of (Matrikulasi / Asasi) at IPTA
  • Offer include degree in IPTA if obtained excellent result in preparation programme (Matrikulasi/Asasi) with minimum requirement of at least CGPA 3.75 or more
  • Should also receive an offer from any IPTA especially in Professionalism (Ikhtisas) and Critical field

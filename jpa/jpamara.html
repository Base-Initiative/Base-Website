JPA-MARA

The JPA-MARA Special Program is a sponsorship program for Bumiputera students with excellent SPM results and pursuing higher education in selected courses.

JPA-MARA Scholarship in a Nutshell

Sponsorship for excellent SPM students to continue their foundation and undergraduate studies in local and international institutions.

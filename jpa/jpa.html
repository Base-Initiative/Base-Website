JPA

JPA Scholarship is a scholarship offered by Malaysia Public Service to the Malaysian nationality who is continuing their studies at locally and overseas universities or colleges.

Scholarships Offered

Types of scholarships offered by JPA ranging from international to local sponsors.

JPA-MARA

The JPA-MARA Special Program is a sponsorship program for Bumiputera students with excellent SPM results and pursuing higher education in selected courses.

Learn More

JPA-JKPJ

The JKPJ scholarship is offered to SPM students to pursue courses related to engineering, social science or science and technology in Japan, Korea, France and Germany.

Learn More

JPA-PPN

The most prestigious scholarship offered by JPA, the Program Penajaan Nasional (PPN) only open to the 20 most outstanding students of Sijil Pelajaran Malaysia (SPM) every year.

Learn More

JPA in a Nutshell

JPA was created to nurture the right talents who will ultimately contribute to the growth of the nation.

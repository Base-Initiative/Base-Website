PESP

Since 1975, Petronas Education Sponsorship Programme (PESP) has awarded teaching Petronas scholarships to more than 36,000 high-level deserving students.

PESP in a Nutshell

The PESP was created to nurture the right talents who will ultimately contribute to the growth of the company and the nation.

All You Need to Know: PESP

  1. UTP:
    • Chemical Engineering, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, Petroleum Geoscience
  2. Overseas:
    • Applied Chemistry, Chemical Engineering, Electrical & Electronic Engineering, Electrical & Electronic Engineering (Instrumentation)
    • Geology/Geophysics, Material Engineering/Material Science, Mechanical Engineering, Petroleum Engineering, Software Engineering
    • Accounting, Actuarial Science, Computer Science, Economics, Law, Mathematics, Psychology, Sociology, Statistics
  1. Malaysian citizens from 17 to 19 years old in 2021
  2. Local Programme: Obtained minimum SPM 8A & CEFR (B1)
  3. Oversea Programme: Obtained minimum SPM 4A+ & 4A & CEFR (C1)>
  4. Engineering: Minimum 9A- in SPM, with minimum A for the following subjects: Chemistry, Physics, Mathematics
  5. Note: Please note that the eligibility criteria for your application may vary depending on your selected discipline (technical or non-technical). For more detailed information, please refer to the PETRONAS education sponsorship application guidelines available at the Online Application System during application period.
For Phase 1 : Scanned copy of applicant’s IC, SPM result, latest picture (preferably passport size)
Fill in the form via Online Application System (OAS)
  • Submit application online
  • Phase 1 : Cambridge Personal Styles Questionnaire (CPSQ) test
  • Phase 2 : Thinking Skills Assessment (TSA) test
  • Phase 3 : Student Potential Assessment (only candidates for overseas sponsorship programme who met the requirements for the TSA
Basic questions :
  • Tell me about yourself.
  • Why did you choose this course?
  • Latest issues ie. Covid 19, Technological Development
